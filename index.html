STRUCTURAL STABILITY

To eliminate settling logs as they age, we construct our homes using a traditional method of framing.
This allows for the natural movement of wood throughout the cabin without causing structural issues or drafts.

IN CASE OF FIRE

Log homes and log cabins built with solid log walls are extremely slow to spread fire compared to wood frame walls.
Since there are no air pockets or cavities in the walls, a fire is usually a surface burn.
Typically contents of the home will spread the fire much faster than the walls.
