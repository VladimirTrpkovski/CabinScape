Our Story

It all began as a little side project back in the fall of 1995. Vladimir Trpkovski (founder and owner) was employed in residential construction, and he was inspired to build a little backyard cabin. Deciding the only way it would develop into anything was if he put his whole heart into it, Dave left his job in February 1995 at the young age of 23 and started CabinScape. He set up shop in a barn, which was located on the same rental property where he lived with his family. But one thing has remained constant through the years: a strong, personal commitment to quality in both product and service. Over time, CabinScape has built the largest network of model log homes in the Europa. In recent years, we have worked hard to develop our website as a comprehensive internet resource for our customers to learn about log homes. As a result, our website has become the most visited web site in the log home industry.

Our dedicated service and skills are reflected through our entire company. CabinScape customers work with a highly experienced “Dream Team” that is made up of our log home consultants, designers, project managers, and technical experts to provide support and guidance every step of the way. Collectively, CabinScape has hundreds of years of experience in crafting quality log cabin kits. Each member of our superb staff takes great personal pride in producing what we enthusiastically believe to be the finest log homes made in Europa today.Today we employ more than 30 employees, and have built homes for 5 countries. Our team is committed to providing cabins and homes with repeatable and dependable quality. We have been richly blessed on this journey, and are excited to see what is yet in store! Come by one of our many locations for more information on how we can help you build your log home dream.
